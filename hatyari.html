🌿 Hatyari Khoh Waterfall

If you're looking for a thrilling and offbeat adventure, Hatyari Waterfall is a must-visit destination. Nestled in the lush green forests of Madhya Pradesh, this stunning waterfall offers breathtaking views, a serene environment, and a touch of mystery.

33.3 Km via Kampel Rd

🌲 Why Visit Hatyari Khoh Waterfall?

▪ Scenic Beauty: The waterfall cascades down rugged cliffs, creating a mesmerizing view surrounded by dense greenery.
▪ Adventure & Trekking: The journey to Hatyari Waterfall involves an exciting trek through forest trails, making it a paradise for adventure seekers.
▪ Peace & Solitude: Unlike commercialized waterfalls, Hatyari Waterfall is less crowded, offering a peaceful retreat into nature.
▪ Myth & Mystery: The name "Hatyari" (meaning ‘Murderous’ in Hindi) adds an element of intrigue, as local legends suggest the place has a mysterious past.

🏞️ Things to Do in Hatyari Waterfall

▪ Trekking & Hiking: Enjoy a refreshing trek through the dense forest trails leading to the waterfall.
▪ Photography: Capture the mesmerizing views of cascading waters, rocky landscapes, and lush greenery.
▪ Picnic & Relaxation: Spend a peaceful time by the waterfall, enjoying nature’s tranquility.
▪ Explore the Legends: Talk to locals and learn about the myths associated with the waterfall’s unique name.

🍁 Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Hatyari Waterfall is during the monsoon and post-monsoon months (July to October). The waterfall is in full flow, and the surrounding landscape turns lush green, enhancing its beauty.

♻️🌿 Safety & Travel Tips

✔ Wear comfortable trekking shoes for the rugged terrain.
✔ Carry drinking water and snacks, as there are limited shops nearby.
✔ Visit in a group for safety, as the area is secluded.
✔ Avoid visiting during heavy rains due to slippery paths and strong water currents.
