🌿 Welcome to Chidiya Bhadak

Chidhiya Bhadak Waterfall is a breathtaking natural attraction located near Indore. Surrounded by lush greenery, rocky terrains, and serene landscapes, this waterfall is a perfect getaway for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and photographers. If you’re looking for an offbeat destination with scenic beauty and a peaceful environment, Chidhiya Bhadak should be on your travel list.

🌲 Why Visit Chidiya Bhadak?

✅Mesmerizing Waterfall – A stunning cascade that flows gracefully through the rocky cliffs.
✅ Lush Greenery & Tranquility – A peaceful retreat away from city noise.
✅Adventure Trekking – A thrilling trek through forested trails leading to the falls.
✅Perfect for Photography – Ideal for capturing nature’s beauty in every frame.

🌿🏞️ Best Things to Do in Chidiya Bhadak

✅Trekking & Hiking – Enjoy an adventurous trek to the falls.
✅Picnic & Relaxation – Perfect for a day out with friends and family.
✅Photography & Videography – Capture stunning waterfalls and natural landscapes.
✅Birdwatching & Wildlife Spotting – Experience the rich biodiversity of the region.

🍁 Best Time to Visit

🌧️Monsoon (July to September) – The waterfall is in full glory, making it the best time to visit.
🌧️ Post-Monsoon (October to December) – The weather remains pleasant, and the surroundings are lush green.

♻️🌿 Safety & Travel Tips

✔ Wear comfortable trekking shoes as the terrain can be rocky and slippery.
✔ Carry snacks and water since there are no major eateries nearby.
✔ Visit early in the morning to enjoy the serene atmosphere.
✔ Avoid visiting during heavy rains as the water currents may become strong.
✔ Do not litter—help preserve the natural beauty of the place.


“While the trek is beginner-friendly, it’s best to go with an experienced guide if you’re visiting for the first time.”

Note: Leave no waste, only footprints! 👣🌿
