🌊 Welcome to Noni Waterfall

Noni Waterfall is a breathtaking natural wonder, offering a perfect escape for those who love serenity, adventure, and picturesque landscapes. Tucked away in lush greenery, this hidden gem is an ideal spot for trekking enthusiasts and nature lovers looking for a peaceful retreat.

40 KM from Indore

🌊 Why Visit Noni waterfall?

▪ Mesmerizing Beauty: Crystal-clear water cascading down rugged rocks amidst dense forests makes Noni Waterfall a spectacular sight.
▪ Perfect for Adventure Seekers: The trek to the waterfall is an exhilarating experience, passing through scenic trails, rich flora, and diverse wildlife.
▪ A Photographer’s Delight: Capture the stunning waterfall, vibrant greenery, and the tranquil atmosphere in your camera.
▪ Peaceful Retreat: Unlike crowded tourist spots, Noni Waterfall remains relatively unexplored, offering a calm and refreshing ambiance.

🌊 Best Time to Visit

▪ The best time to visit is during and after the monsoon season (July to October) when the waterfall is in full flow, surrounded by lush greenery.
▪ Avoid peak summer months, as the water level may be low, and the heat can make trekking uncomfortable.

🌊 How to Reach Noni Waterfall?

▪ Nearest City/Town: The waterfall is located near [Nearest City/Town], making it easily accessible for a weekend getaway.
▪ Transport Options: Visitors can reach Noni Waterfall via public transport, private vehicles, or guided tours. Parking is available at the base of the trek.
▪ Trek Details: The trek is moderately difficult, taking around 1-2 hours through lush trails and rocky paths. Proper trekking gear is recommended.

Things to Keep in Mind

✅ Wear comfortable trekking shoes and carry a raincoat during monsoon.
✅ Bring sufficient water and light snacks.
✅ Be cautious while walking on slippery rocks.
✅ Avoid littering and respect nature.

Nearby Attractions

▪ Sunset Point: A stunning viewpoint offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.
▪ Ancient Shiva Temple: A revered temple located a short distance from the waterfall, adding a spiritual touch to your journey.
▪ Eco Park: A perfect picnic spot with well-maintained gardens and a serene ambiance.
▪ Hidden Caves: A set of small caves near the waterfall, rumored to have been used by ancient travelers and monks.
